Sidecoin is an open-source wallet for the upcoming eCash hard fork
(block ~964,000, August 21 2026).

It supports BIP-300 Hashrate Escrows and BIP-301 Blind Merged Mining,
enabling trustless sidechains secured by Bitcoin's full SHA-256d hashrate.

Features:
• 8 sidechains at launch (Thunder, zSide, BitNames, BitAssets, Photon, Truthcoin, CoinShift, and more)
• BIP-300 deposit and withdrawal management
• QR code scanning for payments
• Biometric authentication
• SPV lightweight mode

Sidecoin is free software released under the MIT license.
No tracking, no ads, no data collection.
